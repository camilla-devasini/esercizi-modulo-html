Boost your focus and productivity

Do you ever feel overwhelmed by things that need to get done? Who has never experienced something like this? We will guide you through our guidelines in order to help you to keep everything simple and under control. Steve Jobs once said at Interview with Business Week, 1998 Focus and simplicity. Simple can be harder than complex: you have to work hard to get your thinking clean to make it simple.

The following steps have been suggested by our team and will help you to balance both your working and personal life, since we believe that a huge amount of our energy should be saved for our personal interests and to what we truly love.

Set daily goals

The most important thing it's being well organized. The advice here is about listing all the things you need to do, dividing them by urgency and assigning them a value on the basis of their importance. In this way you will have a clear idea about what you need to focus on at first. We suggest to write everything down on paper too.

Adopt the 2 minute rule

According to this simple rule, if a task requires less then 2 minutes to be done, you should prioritize it. in this way you get rid of lots of activities and you will be then free to focus on the big tasks. In order to deepen this concept, read David Allen consultant's book

Use the pomodoro technique

The pomorodo techinque has been invented by Francesco Cirillo in the late 1980s, consisting in setting a timer for your tasks, usually 25 minutes. The pomodoro indeed, defines a time interval. in this way you will have a clear division of your time for being productive and relaxed. Click here to set your pomofocus timer.
